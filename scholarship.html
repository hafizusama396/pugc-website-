  • Scholarship

Punjab University offers various scholarships, including Need-based, Disabled students, Ph.D., M.Phil, Sports, and International scholarships, with benefits like stipends, fee waivers, and research grants.

SCHOLARSHIPS NEWS

Punjab University has taken significant steps to support its students financially. The university has approved scholarships for over 1300 students, including 480 eligible students of the 2023-24 session and 826 eligible students of previous sessions. Furthermore, the university has launched the Punjab Honahar Merit Scholarship, increasing the number of scholarships from 4000 to 25000. This initiative benefits deserving students with 100% fee payment and stipends, enabling them to focus on their academic pursuits without financial burdens. Additionally, the government has introduced a Free iPad Scheme for students, providing them with the necessary digital resources to excel in their studies. These initiatives demonstrate the university's commitment to supporting its students and promoting academic excellence.

CHEMICAL ENGINEERING

    The Institute of Chemical Engineering & Technology (ICET) at Punjab University offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs. ICET focuses on process design, development, and operation, with research collaborations and digital access to resources. Research programs include Master of Engineering (Food Technology), Master of Technology (Polymer), and Doctoral Programme.

MUSIC & ARTS

The University of Punjab's Institute of Art and Design and Department of Music offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The institute focuses on fine arts, graphic design, and musicology, with facilities including a music library, recording studio, and art galleries. The music department offers specializations in classical, semi-classical, and light music, with a focus on Indian classical music traditions.

GRAPHICS DESIGN

Graphic Design is a craft that combines art and technology to communicate ideas. The University of Punjab's Institute of Art and Design offers a Bachelor of Fine Arts (4 Years Program) in Graphic Design , focusing on visual communication, typography, and digital media.
