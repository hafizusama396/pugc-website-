Punjab University Gujranwala Campus is a satellite campus of University of the Punjab, Lahore, located in Gujranwala, Punjab, Pakistan, with 81 Kanals of land and 37 classrooms.

ABOUT OUR JOURNY

Established in 2006 as a satellite campus of the University of the Punjab, Lahore, the campus has come a long way in providing quality education to the people of Gujranwala and surrounding areas.

The campus has made significant strides in terms of infrastructure development, faculty recruitment, and curriculum design. Today, it offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as business, commerce, and computer science. The campus is committed to promoting research and innovation in the region and has established partnerships with local industries and organizations to provide students with opportunities for internships, job placements, and collaborative research projects. Through its "Our Journey" initiative, the campus aims to inspire and empower students to become active contributors to the region's economic and social development.
